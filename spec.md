# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Move the Real, Hourly, and Year mode buttons along with their time display from the bottom-right floating panel into the Sun Effects section of the World Controls panel.

**Planned changes:**
- Relocate the Real, Hourly, and Year buttons into the Sun Effects section of the World Controls panel as a cohesive sub-section.
- Relocate the corresponding time display into the Sun Effects section alongside the buttons.
- Remove the bottom-right floating panel entirely from the DOM (no placeholder or ghost element left behind).
- Preserve all existing mode-switching functionality and time display update behavior.
- Keep all other UI elements, panels, and the 3D globe unchanged.

**User-visible outcome:** The sun simulation mode buttons and time display are now found inside the Sun Effects section of the World Controls panel, and the bottom-right floating panel no longer appears on screen.
